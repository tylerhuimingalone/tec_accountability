If you or a loved one is currently experiencing domestic violence, you can contact the National Domestic Violence Hotline here: https://ncadv.org/get-help

If you have also experienced abuse by a priest or other religious leader, you can contact the Survivors Network of those Abused by Priests here: https://www.snapnetwork.org/

If you are an Episcopalian who stands with us, we humbly ask you to stop financially donating to The Episcopal Church, until the organization has clearly demonstrated it will not silence abuse survivors and will repent from its own evident, public mistakes. If you are asked why, we hope you will share our story. If you have something you would like to share with TEC leadership, relevant contact information can be found below. We encourage you to share your thoughts and feelings, as well as the impact TEC's inaction in this matter has on you and your support of TEC. We encourage you to share with TEC leadership, as well as your local Clergy. We have included email templates below for your convinience.
